# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by telecom companies. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project focuses on analyzing customer churn patterns and identifying the key factors that contribute to customer attrition.

Using Tableau, an interactive dashboard was developed to examine customer behavior, contract preferences, service subscriptions, payment methods, and demographic characteristics. The objective was to uncover insights that can help improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

---

## Business Problem

The telecom company has been experiencing customer attrition, leading to a decline in revenue and customer lifetime value. The organization wants to understand:

- Which customer groups are most likely to churn
- How contract types influence customer retention
- The impact of internet services on churn behavior
- Whether payment methods affect customer loyalty
- The overall revenue impact of customer churn

The insights generated from this analysis can support data-driven retention strategies.

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer-level information, including:

- Customer Demographics
- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Payment Method
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges
- Tenure
- Churn Status

The dataset consists of over 7,000 customer records and provides sufficient information to analyze customer retention trends.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate (%)
- Average Monthly Charges
- Revenue Lost Due to Churn

---

## Dashboard Features

### Customer Churn Overview
Provides a high-level view of customer churn and retention performance.

### Churn Rate by Contract Type
Analyzes churn behavior across different contract categories.

### Churn Rate by Tenure Group
Examines how customer tenure impacts the likelihood of churn.

### Churn Rate by Internet Service
Evaluates churn rates among different internet service types.

### Churn Rate by Payment Method
Identifies payment methods associated with higher customer attrition.

### Customer Distribution
Visualizes the proportion of retained and churned customers.

### Interactive Filters

- Contract Type
- Internet Service
- Payment Method
- Gender
- Senior Citizen Status

---

## Key Insights

- Customers on Month-to-Month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn rates than customers on long-term contracts.
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave the company.
- Certain payment methods show a higher concentration of churned customers.
- Internet service type plays a notable role in customer retention.
- Customer churn contributes substantially to revenue loss.
- Long-term contract customers demonstrate stronger loyalty and retention.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Promote long-term contracts through targeted incentives and discounts.
2. Implement retention campaigns for newly acquired customers during the early stages of their subscription.
3. Improve service offerings for customer segments with elevated churn rates.
4. Develop personalized retention strategies based on customer behavior and service usage patterns.
5. Monitor high-risk customer groups proactively to reduce future churn.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data visualization can be used to identify customer churn patterns and support business decision-making. The analysis highlights key retention drivers and provides actionable insights that can help telecom companies improve customer satisfaction, reduce churn, and protect revenue.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to interactively explore customer behavior and gain a deeper understanding of factors influencing customer retention.
